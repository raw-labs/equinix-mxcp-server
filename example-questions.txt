## Example Questions for Equinix Billing Analytics

### Overview & Discovery
- What IBX sites does Vodafone Ireland have, and how much do we spend at each?
- What product categories are we billed for, and what's the total spend per category?
- Give me a high-level breakdown of our billing by product category and IBX location.

### Spend Analysis
- What is our total monthly spend, and how has it trended over time?
- Which month had the highest total billing amount?
- How does our Space spend compare to our Power spend month over month?
- What percentage of our total spend goes to Interconnection vs Space?

### Pricing & Benchmarking
- How do our actual billed prices for Space and Power compare to the 2020 rate card?
- Are we paying above or below the rate card at each Dublin IBX?
- Which IBX has the biggest gap between billed price and rate card pricing?
- How have our unit prices evolved across different contract vintages?
- Are newer contracts priced higher or lower than older ones?

### Contract Management
- What does our contract portfolio look like across all IBX sites?
- Which agreements are expiring before the end of 2026?
- What is our total financial exposure from contracts expiring in the next 12 months?
- How many contracts are marked as new activity vs existing?

### Site-Specific Analysis
- Show me all billing details for DB2.
- What are the Power charges at DB3?
- Which site has the most billing line items?
- Compare the spend across DB1, DB2, and DB3.
