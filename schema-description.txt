## Equinix Billing Data Schema

You have access to Vodafone Ireland's Equinix data center billing data across three Dublin IBX sites (DB1, DB2, DB3), covering four product categories: Space, Power, Interconnection, and Services. The data includes contract lifecycle information for pricing evolution analysis, renewal planning, and rate card benchmarking. Additionally, the full 2020 global rate card and the March 2025 DB2 replacement service order are available as reference data.

### Data Tables

**load_billing_data** — Billing transaction lines from the TDR Excel sheet.
Columns:
- trx_number (string): Invoice transaction number.
- trx_date (date): Transaction date.
- currency (string): Invoice currency code (EUR).
- product_category (string): One of Power, Interconnection, Services, Space.
- charge_type (string): Charge type label (RC, NRC, Usage, Reseller Discount).
- charge_type_clean (string): Normalized charge type classification.
- ibx (string): IBX data center location code (DB1, DB2, DB3).
- quantity (number): Billed quantity.
- unit_price (number): Price per unit.
- line_amount (number): Net line amount before GST.
- gst_amount (number): GST amount applied.
- total_amount (number): Line total including GST.
- description (string): Line item description from invoice.
- product_offering (string): Product offering code.
- product_elements (string): Product element code.
- billing_agreement (string): Billing agreement identifier.
- serial_number (string): Asset or serial number.
- recurring_charge_from (date): Recurring charge period start.
- recurring_charge_to (date): Recurring charge period end.
- customer_name (string): Customer account name.
- billing_agreement_start (date): Billing agreement start date.
- billing_agreement_end (date): Billing agreement end date.
- service_start_date (date): Date the service began.
- installation_date (date): Date equipment was installed.
- contract_duration (number, nullable): Contract duration in months (~75% populated).
- new_activity (string): New (Y) or existing (N) activity flag.
- sales_order_number (string): Sales order number.

### Rate Card Reference Tables (2020 Global Rate Card)

**rate_card_dublin** — Dublin-only rate card pricing (4 rows: DB1–DB4). Used by the compare_to_rate_card tool.
Columns: ibx, min_density_kva, price_per_kva_eur, cabinet_install_nrc_eur, cage_install_nrc_eur, smart_hands_hourly_eur, effective_date.

**rate_card_space_power** — Global Space and Power pricing for all 200 IBXs worldwide (200 rows across Americas, APAC, EMEA). Prices in local currencies; empty price_per_kva means ICB (individual case basis).
Columns: country, region (Americas|APAC|EMEA), metro, ibx, min_density_kva, price_per_kva, currency, effective_date.

**rate_card_smart_hands** — Global Smart Hands hourly rates by country (25 rows).
Columns: country, region (Americas|APAC|EMEA), rate_per_hour, currency, effective_date.

**rate_card_installation_nrc** — Cabinet and cage installation NRCs by country/region (29 rows). Some countries have site-specific pricing tiers.
Columns: country, region, sites, cabinet_nrc, cage_nrc, currency, effective_date.

**rate_card_interconnection_americas** — Americas interconnection product pricing for US, Canada, Brazil, Colombia (48 rows). Products include Cross Connect, Extended Fiber, Metro Connect, Internet Exchange, Cloud Exchange, Equinix Connect, and IP Allocation.
Columns: product, type, us_mrc, us_nrc, ca_mrc, ca_nrc, br_mrc, br_nrc, co_mrc, co_nrc, effective_date.

**rate_card_power_circuit_nrc_americas** — Americas power circuit installation NRCs by circuit type (11 rows). Covers AC single/three phase at various voltages and DC 48v.
Columns: circuit_type, us_all_ibxs, us_da7_dc10_dc12_dc15, ca_all, br_all, co_all, effective_date.

**rate_card_power_circuit_nrc_apac** — APAC power circuit installation NRCs (10 rows). Covers Hong Kong, Singapore, Australia, Japan, China, South Korea.
Columns: circuit_type, amps, hk_nrc, sg_nrc, au_nrc, jp_nrc, cn_nrc, kr_nrc, effective_date.

**rate_card_power_circuit_nrc_emea** — EMEA power circuit installation NRCs by country (16 rows). Voltage/amperage combinations across 16 European/Middle East countries.
Columns: country, sites, 230v_16amp, 230v_32amp, 230v_63amp, 400v_16amp, 400v_32amp, 400v_63amp, currency, effective_date.

### Service Order Reference Tables (March 2025 DB2 Replacement Order)

**service_order_db2_2025** — Consolidated key line items (4 rows: Cage, Power, Cross Connect, Innerducts).
Columns: product_code, description, ibx, quantity, uom, unit_mrc_eur, discount_pct, effective_mrc_eur, effective_date, initial_term_months, product_category.

**service_order_db2_2025_terms** — Commercial terms as key-value pairs (19 rows). Includes order number, account details, agreement number, term lengths, price increase terms, grand total MRC/NRC, and signature dates.
Columns: term_name, term_value.

**service_order_db2_2025_ramp** — Ramp schedules for Cage and AC Power (4 rows: 3 cage stages + 1 power stage). Shows progressive capacity/pricing over the 60-month term.
Columns: product, ramp_stage, period_months, start_date, ramp_draw_cap_pct, mrc_eur, max_allowed_kva.

**service_order_db2_2025_additions** — Section A: Product Additions (6 rows). New products being added including cage, cabinets, and AC power with pricing and discounts.
Columns: product_code, description, qty, uom, unit_nrc, unit_mrc, total_nrc, total_mrc, discount_nrc_pct, discount_mrc_pct, effective_date.

**service_order_db2_2025_deletions** — Section B: Product Deletions (7 rows). Products being removed including old cage, cabinets, metered power, with serial numbers and current pricing.
Columns: product_code, description, qty, uom, serial_number, total_nrc, current_mrc, amended_mrc, effective_date.

**service_order_db2_2025_changes** — Section C: Product/Element Changes (42 rows). Cross connect and innerduct price updates showing current vs amended MRC. Most cross connects unified to EUR 125.00/month from various legacy prices.
Columns: product_code, description, serial_number, qty, uom, action, total_nrc, current_mrc, amended_mrc, effective_date.

**service_order_db2_2025_continuing** — Section D: Continuing Services (8 rows). Intra-customer cross connects and Smart Hands support plans continuing at zero cost.
Columns: product_code, description, serial_number, qty, uom, unit_nrc, unit_mrc, total_nrc, total_mrc, effective_date.

**service_order_db2_2025_section_totals** — Section totals summary (4 rows). Grand Total: MRC EUR 149,827.90, NRC EUR 0.00.
Columns: section (A|B|C|D), description, total_nrc, total_mrc.

**service_order_db2_2025_order_refs** — Order reference mapping (3 rows). Shows which old orders are being replaced by this replacement order, by product family.
Columns: ibx, product_family, order_reference, new_order_reference.
